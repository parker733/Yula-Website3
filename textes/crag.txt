CONCEPT -
<br/>
<br/>The illustrated campaign for Craigslist, promoting the idea “ONE FOR ALL”. 
The concept was based on the biggest advantage of Craigslist, diversity. Through the one giant powerful platform, users can access to thousands of information and services for all kinds of needs. The text “ONE FOR ALL” represents one platform for all services all over the world. 
<br/>
<br/>DESIGN -
<br/>
<br/>
The campaign introduces a new character, the Craigslist’s man, aims to bring a more interactive and playful image to the brand. Animations and illustrations serve the purpose of reinforcing the concept of diversity. Color palette uses the brand-representative blue, pairing with a energetic yellow to light up the design.
