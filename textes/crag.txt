CONCEPT -
<br/>
<br/>This is an illustrated campaign for Craigslist, promoting the idea of “ONE FOR ALL”. The concept was based on the biggest advantage of Craigslist: Diversity. Under the household name of Craigslist, users will be able to access to thousands of information and services for all kinds of needs. The copy “ONE FOR ALL” represents one platform for all services all over the world.  
<br/>
<br/>DESIGN -
<br/>
<br/>
The campaign introduces a new mascot of Craigslist, which aims to bring a more playful image to the brand. Animations and illustrations serve the purpose of reinforcing the concept of diversity. The color palette borrows the representative blue, pairing with an energetic yellow to light up the design.
