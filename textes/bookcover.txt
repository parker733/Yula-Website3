CONCEPT -
<br/>
<br/>
Printed covers for interior designer Yuxing Ling. Each book consists of one project, developed between 2017 and 2018. It was important to show Ling’s dynamic approach of her study, hence the images contrast with a systematic layout.   
<br/>
<br/>ACTIVE RHYTHM -
<br/>
<br/>
Active Rhythm is the 2nd project in Ling’s design series. Giving the name she aims to activate a repeated pattern of reflections and movement.
<br/>
<br/>A SHELTER -
<br/>
<br/> 
A Shelter is the 3rd project in the design series. The cover image is photographed to express the real face of a structure under the surface material and decorations.
