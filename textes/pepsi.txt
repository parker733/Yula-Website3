PEPSI BLUE RABBIT -
<br/>
<br/>Pepsi Seeing the World is an Instagram campaign collaborated by Pepsi and the famous artist Esoj Luna. This collaboration introduced the Pepsi Blue Rabbit.
<br/>
<br/>CONCEPT -
<br/>
<br/>The campaign exists on Instagram as a public account, providing a platform for people to post creative images and stories about Pepsi. An IG filter is also established by Pepsi. When sharing a photo or video to your story, you can add the Pepsi Blue Rabbit stickers to express yourself in fun and unique ways. Tag Pepsi Seeing the World will also provide you chances to win the personalized limited Pepsi bottles.
