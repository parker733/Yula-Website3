ABOUT -
<br/>
<br/> Identity design for Fire Tongue Reward Club, a worldwide royal club specializes in spicy cuisines. The club is established based on the idea of “tasting fire”. It connects spicy food aficionados and restaurants all over the world, provides a booking and reservation system to ensure your access to the hottest table in your area.
<br/>
<br/>GOAL -
<br/>
<br/>
The goal is to create an identity system for the club, communicate the idea of “tasting fire” through visual languages. The design includes a logo, invitations, and print collateral. 
<br/>
<br/>LOGO -
<br/>
<br/>
The logo is created based on the four initials of the club, FTRC. It is designed with sharp angles, aimed to communicate the idea of fire tasting stimulation.
<br/>
<br/>INVITATION -
<br/>
<br/>
The invitation includes a letter and a membership card. It could be printed on paper or sent via email. The design represents the core concept of the club - “tasting fire”. The color palette is inspired by the colors of fire. Gradients and patterns are used to create movement, convey a powerful and mystery tonality.
<br/>
<br/>POSTER MAP -
<br/>
<br/>
The poster map is a printed-based design, it will also be sent to the new members. The poster is designed in the form of a world map, representing the club’s diverse and powerful connections all over the world. Representative places with spectacular spicy cuisines are highlighted in the map, served as a guide tool for aficionados to explore.
