CONCEPT -
<br/>
<br/>Op Effects use color, light and patterns to create illusional images. It occurs because our brain is trying to interpret what we see and what we think we are seeing. In a way, optical illusion tricks our brains and leads our eye to move.
<br/>
<br/>DESIGN -
<br/>
<br/>
THE MOVING EYE is an exhibition design in the subject of optical illusion. The exhibition includes a collection of artworks from representative op artists and designers from the 1950s to present. Today we juxtapose works from its founding era with comtemporary design, connect history to present through this exhibition, THE MOVING EYE.
